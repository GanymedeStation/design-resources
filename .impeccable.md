## Design Context

### Users
People browsing the Awesome Design Resources list, typically designers and product builders looking for tools, inspiration, and references. They want to search quickly, scan confidently, and land on trustworthy external resources.

### Brand Personality
Polished, dependable, and slightly editorial. The interface should feel curated rather than noisy, with a calm technical edge and a clear sense of craft.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark, refined, and resource-focused. Use sharp contrast, restrained accent color, strong typography, and intentional spacing. Keep the visual language consistent with the existing app: premium but practical, not flashy.

### Design Principles
- Make the primary action obvious and keep secondary actions quiet.
- Preserve strong readability and clear hierarchy at every size.
- Use compact, deliberate layouts instead of generic card-heavy compositions.
- Treat empty, error, and fallback states as part of the product, not an afterthought.
- Keep accessibility and responsive behavior intact as the design evolves.
